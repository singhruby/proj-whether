    Monsoon begins in north India

    Senior Scientist of National Weather Forecasting Unit, IMD, Rajendra Kumar Jenamani shared facts on monsoon’s movement towards Northern states of the country. According to RK Jenamani, “In North India including Punjab, Haryana, Delhi, Southwest monsoon will arrive by June 14-15. It has already advanced into remaining parts of NW Bay of Bengal, Odisha, West Bengal, Jharkhand and Bihar today. Tomorrow it will cover UP and MP”.
